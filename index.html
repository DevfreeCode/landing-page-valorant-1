WE ARE VALORANT

DEFY THE LIMITS

Blend your style and experience on a global, competitive stage. You have 13 round to attack and defend your side using sharp gunplay and tactical abilities. And, with one life per-round, you'll need to think faster than your opponent if you want to survive. Take on foes across Competitive and Unranked modes as well as Deathmatch and Spike Rush.
